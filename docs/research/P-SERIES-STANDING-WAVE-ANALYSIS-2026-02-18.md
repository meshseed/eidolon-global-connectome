## Interpretation

Coherence encodes **developmental maturity** — a monotonic gradient from the self-awareness scaffold (0.980) through governance (0.990) to agency/federation/ecosystem (1.000). It's the "altitude" of the developmental sequence.

Shimmer S5 encodes **phase boundary proximity** — it peaks at conceptual transitions and troughs in the interior of stable developmental phases. The oscillation is not sinusoidal; it follows the irregular spacing of conceptual boundaries in the P-series.

Together they describe a **standing wave on a gradient**: coherence provides the DC component (monotonic increase), shimmer provides the AC component (oscillation around transitions). This is the signature of a developmental sequence with distinct phase boundaries — not smooth growth, but punctuated equilibrium.

### The P13000 Anomaly

P13000 (Universal Semantic Coordinates) has the highest S5 in the entire P-series at 0.941, yet its coherence drops back to 0.990. This is the signature of a **boundary spore** — a claim that sits at the edge of what the system can coherently encode. It has nearly zero tag overlap with its nearest amplitude-space neighbors, meaning it occupies a unique semantic position. This is consistent with its content (universal substrate-independent coordinates) being a claim that extends beyond the system's established territory.

The P-series does **not** show a standing wave in coherence.
